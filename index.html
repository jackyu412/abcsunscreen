What We Do

New Mexico is a high risk place for skin cancer, with its high altitute and sunny days. This is why the use of sunscreen is extremely important, and whilst many New Mexicans use sunscreen, many are uneducated about it's harm, and some people have difficult accessing it due to it's high price.

Our plan comprises of two parts: providing sunscreen and educating and promoting its use. We can do this by setting up free sunscreen stands during events such as outdoor swimming meets, track meets, and more outdoor events. At each sunscreen stand, we have free sunscreen along with brochures helping spread sunscreen awareness
